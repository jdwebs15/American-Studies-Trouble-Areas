Questions

1. Which principle divides power between national and state governments? A. limited government
B. federalism
C. checks and balances
D. judicial review

B. Federalists
C. Populists

D. Mayflower Compact

B. trial by jury
C. unlimited slavery
D. royal taxation

B. amendments expanding voting rights
C. rise of corporations

B. shorter workdays
C. safer working conditions

B. Spanish-American War

B. Woodrow Wilson
C. Herbert Hoover
D. Harry Truman

B. debate over the Treaty of Versailles

B. suburbanization
C. Japanese internment
D. bank failures

B. speculation with borrowed money
C. wartime mobilization
D. stronger labor unions

B. Supreme Court
C. Federal Reserve System

B. Reconstruction
C. Progressive Era
D. Gilded Age

B. failure of international peace agreements

B. avoided industrial production

B. Neutrality Act
