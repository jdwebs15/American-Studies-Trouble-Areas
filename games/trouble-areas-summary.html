Teacher Summary

Most-Missed Trouble Areas Snapshot

This build was anchored to the lowest-consensus questions in the uploaded Google Forms response exports. The biggest pattern was not simple recall failure. The biggest pattern was confusion with classification, cause/effect, and multi-select evaluation.

Highest-confusion clusters

  • Post-WWI peace efforts (select three)
  • Labor union goals (select all)
  • Progressive reforms (select all)
  • Causes of World War II (select three)
  • Federalism and ratification debates
  • New Deal expansion and containment policy identification
  • Civil rights actions and housing discrimination

Instructional read on the data

  • Students confuse examples, definitions, and outcomes.
  • Students struggle when more than one answer is correct.
  • Part A / Part B questions reveal weak transfer from passage to concept.
  • Application questions break down faster than vocabulary questions.
